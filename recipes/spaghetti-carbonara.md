---
title: Spaghetti Carbonara
---

## Ingredients

- 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
- 200g (7 oz) guanciale or pancetta, diced
- 4 large eggs
- 100g (1 cup) Pecorino Romano, finely grated
- 50g (1/2 cup) Parmesan, finely grated
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt (for pasta water)

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente; reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
2. While pasta cooks, sauté guanciale in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.
3. Whisk together eggs, Pecorino, and Parmesan in a bowl. Season generously with black pepper.
4. Add drained hot pasta to the skillet with the guanciale (off the heat).
5. Pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing quickly and adding pasta water a splash at a time to create a creamy sauce.
6. The residual heat should cook the eggs — do not return the pan to the stove or the eggs will scramble.
7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
