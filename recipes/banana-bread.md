---
title: Banana Bread
---

## Ingredients

- 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9×5 inch loaf pan.
2. Mix melted butter into mashed bananas in a large bowl.
3. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla.
4. Add baking soda and salt; mix well.
5. Fold in flour until just combined — do not overmix.
6. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
7. Bake 55–65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
8. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a rack to cool completely before slicing.
